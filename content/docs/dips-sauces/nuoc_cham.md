## Ingredients:

* 2 tbsp sugar
* 2 tbsp lime juice
* 2 tbsp fish sauce
* 2 tbsp water
* 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
* 1-2 Thai red chili, sliced thinly

## Directions:

1. Mix all of the ingredients together
